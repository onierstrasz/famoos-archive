These pages consist of an archive of the SCG FAMOOS pages. The project formally ended in August 2000. The final result of the project is the FAMOOS Reengineering Handbook. This material has been completely revised, extended and rewritten in the form of a book entitled Object-Oriented Reengineering Patterns, to appear in the Spring of 2002.

Last updated: 05 March 2002

 

Last revision: March, 15th 2001

SCG / FAMOOS / Resources

This document contains a number of pointers to various resources that may be helpful in the reengineering of object-oriented software. These resources are gathered as part of the FAMOOS Esprit Project.

Note that the page is in low-maintenance mode as the FAMOOS project is over.
