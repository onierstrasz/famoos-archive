Workshop Program

The workshop program is divided in two half days. During the morning, each position paper will be presented by one of its authors. In the afternoon, we will proceed with an in depth discussion of some topics raised in the morning presentations.

Concerning the presentation of position papers.

  • A presentation takes 15 minutes and an additional 5 minutes of question time.
  • All people present in the workshop are expected to have read the position papers (it is a *work*shap after all, isn't it ?).
  • Each presenter will at least include one slide which raises some questions and issues to work on during the afternoon.
